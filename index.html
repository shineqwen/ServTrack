FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ServTrack

What exactly is ServTrack?

ServTrack is an all-in-one car companion app built specifically for Irish drivers. It combines smart reminders, a garage finder, live fuel prices, parking, NCT prep, penalty point tracking, insurance reminders, and a digital service logbook — all in one place. Think of it as having a knowledgeable friend who knows your car inside out.

How much does it cost?

€0.99 per month after a 1-month free trial. No credit card required to start. One plan, every feature included — no hidden tiers, no ads, no data selling. That's less than €12 a year. A single missed NCT costs €55.

What makes it different from other car apps?

Most car apps treat every vehicle the same. ServTrack knows your specific engine variant, its known faults, and adjusts service intervals based on how you actually drive. A school-run parent gets different advice to a motorway commuter — even if they own the same car. Plus, it's built for Ireland: NCT rules, Irish fuel networks, Dublin clamping zones, Irish penalty point law.

Is the garage finder live?

At launch, the garage finder shows verified garages near you with ratings, reviews, and price ranges. Direct instant booking through ServTrack is coming soon — we're onboarding garages now. When it's ready, you'll be able to book with one tap, no phone calls needed.

What about my data and privacy?

We collect the minimum data needed to run the service: your car details, name, email, and driving style. No location tracking stored on our servers, no data selling, no advertising. All data is hosted in the EU and fully GDPR compliant. You can export or delete your data at any time.

Can I use it if I drive an EV?

Absolutely. EV drivers are fully supported from day one. The fuel tracker switches to EV charging mode showing nearby chargers, and service intervals are adjusted for electric drivetrains — no oil changes, but tyre rotation, brake fluid, and cabin filter reminders still apply.
