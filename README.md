# ServTrack 🚗

> *Finally, someone who knows your car.*

ServTrack is a car maintenance platform launching in Dublin, Ireland — Autumn 2026. We combine manufacturer data, known model faults, and personal service history to give drivers intelligent, car-specific advice. Think less "generic reminder app", more "mechanic friend always in your pocket."

---

## What ServTrack does

- **Model-aware insights** — knows your exact make, model, and engine. Surfaces known issues (BMW oil consumption, Ford timing chains, diesel DPF problems) specific to your car, not generic advice.
- **Proactive reminders** — NCT, oil changes, tax, timing belts — alerts based on your actual mileage and service history, not just calendar dates.
- **Trusted garage booking** — verified local garages, upfront pricing, real reviews. Book in seconds, no phone calls.
- **Full service history** — every job logged, timestamped, and mileage-stamped. Invaluable when selling your car.
